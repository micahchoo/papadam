Media Fragment Annotator

Participatory Listening and Sense Making of Archives (PLASMA) is a project of Janastu collective – who explore inclusive open source technology solutions. Using Plasma, communities can archive media, annotate fragments, discover relationships and share their knowledge.

Follow Plasma development & other projects at open.janastu.org/projects.

Janastu’s online instance is at stories.janastu.org and offline one at papad.iruway.in (reachable from COWMesh).

Typical use cases are: offline archives, oral histories, and media libraries.
