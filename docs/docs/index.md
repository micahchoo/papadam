# papadam

papadam is a community media annotation, media-to-media dialogue, and exhibit platform
for low-connectivity, low-literacy communities.

Hard fork of [PLASMA/papad](https://gitlab.com/servelots/papad) by [Janastu/Servelots](https://janastu.org).

## What it does

- Upload audio, video, and images to a shared archive
- Annotate media with text, images, audio replies, or video replies
- Time-anchor annotations to specific segments of a recording
- Annotate collaboratively and offline (CRDT sync)
- Thread replies across media items — annotate someone's spoken story with your own recording
- Build curated exhibits from the archive and publish them publicly
- Each community group has its own customisable workspace

## Who it is for

Communities, researchers, archivists, and educators working with oral histories,
participatory video, and local knowledge — particularly where connectivity is unreliable
and reading/writing may not be the primary mode of participation.
